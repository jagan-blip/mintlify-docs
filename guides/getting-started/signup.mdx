## Pre-requisites for Sign Up

### **License Key**

You'll need a valid License Key to create your Turbodev account and access the platform.

## Sign Up Process

### **Step 1: Enter Your Credentials**

- Enter your email address
- Enter the License Key provided to you

![Turbodev Account Signup](</images/signup/Turbodev Account signup.png>)

### **Step 2: Add Personal Details**

- Organization Name: Enter your company or business name
- Your Name: Provide your full name
- Password: Create a secure password for your account

![Personal Details](</images/signup/personal details.png>)

### **Step 3: Create Your Workspace**

- Enter your Workspace Name (this will be your main working environment in Turbodev)

![Create Workspace](</images/signup/Create workspace.png>)

Once completed, you'll have full access to your Turbodev dashboard and can begin connecting your WhatsApp number.
