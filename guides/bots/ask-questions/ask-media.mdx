#### Overview

The **Ask Media** card allows you to request media files from customers during a conversation. This can include documents, images, videos, or audio files, making it easy to collect important information directly within the chat.

![Ask Media](</images/bot/ask card/ask media.png>)

#### Question Text

This is the message shown to the customer requesting the media. Keep it short, clear, and specific so users understand exactly what they need to upload.
