## WhatsApp Number and Account Identification

Your WhatsApp business number comes with associated identifiers for communication and account management:

![Number](</images/connect your whatsapp/number.png>)

1. **Connected WhatsApp Number:** Your active business phone number linked to Turbodev
2. **Turbodev Account ID:** A unique identifier assigned to your account for identification and platform communication
3. **Connection Status:** Real-time status indicator showing whether your WhatsApp API is operational

## Manage Your WhatsApp Business Profile in Turbodev

To access and update your profile information, follow these steps:

**Step 1: Access Profile Settings**

Log in to your Turbodev account with your credentials. Navigate to **Settings** and select **WhatsApp Channel**.

![Channel](</images/connect your whatsapp/channel.png>)

**Step 2: Update Profile Details**

In the **Profile** section, you'll find options to manage and customize your business information:

![Profile](</images/connect your whatsapp/profile.png>)

- **Profile Picture:** Upload or update your business logo to create a recognizable visual identity for your brand
- **Address:** Add or edit your business location to help customers find your physical presence
- **Business Description:** Write a clear and compelling summary of what your business offers
- **Email Address:** Provide an email contact for customers who prefer written communication
- **Industry Vertical:** Select the industry or sector your business operates in, helping customers and the platform understand your business category
- **Website URLs:** Add links to your website, allowing customers to explore your products, services, and learn more about your business
- **Tagline:** Create a memorable tagline that captures your business's unique value or mission
