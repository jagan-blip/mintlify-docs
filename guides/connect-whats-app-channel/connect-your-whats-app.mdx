## Follow the steps below:

To begin the process, click on the "Connect Whatsapp" button to start linking your WhatsApp Channel with Turbodev.

<Tip>
  **Important:** Make sure all pre-requisites are ready before connecting your number. [Review pre-requisites here](#)
</Tip>

### **Connection Steps**

### **Step 1: Select Business Assets to Share with Turbodev**

Choose which business assets you want to use with Turbodev. You can either use existing assets or create new ones.

- **Business Portfolio:** Select your existing Business Portfolio from the dropdown,

  If you're new, create a new WhatsApp Business Account
- **WhatsApp Business Account:** Choose an existing account or create a new WhatsApp Business Account

![Select Asset](</images/connect your whatsapp/select asset.png>)

### **Step 2: Enter Your Business Information**

- If you already have a WhatsApp Business Account, select it from the list
- If you're new, create a new WhatsApp Business Account

![Enter Details](</images/connect your whatsapp/enter details.png>)

### **Step 3: Set Up Your WhatsApp Business Profile**

Create your WhatsApp Business profile with information that will be visible to your customers when they view your business profile on WhatsApp.

### **Step 4: Add and Verify Your Phone Number**

- Enter the mobile number you want to connect
- Verify the number using the one-time password (OTP) sent to that number

![Add Number](</images/connect your whatsapp/add number.png>)

### **Step 5: Wait for Account Creation**

Allow 3-5 minutes for your WhatsApp Business Account to be created and configured.

### **Step 6: Connect to Turbodev**

Once your account is created, it will automatically connect to Turbodev. This process takes approximately 30-45 seconds.

### **Step 7: Register Your Channel**

After the connection is established, you'll be prompted to register your channel within Turbodev.

**Congratulations!** Your WhatsApp number is now successfully connected with Turbodev. You're ready to start automating conversations and engaging with your customers through the platform.
