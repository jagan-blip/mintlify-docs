## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- Access to **Meta Business Manager**
- Admin permissions for your WhatsApp Business Account
- A valid payment method (Credit/Debit card)
- Billing information ready

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Log in to WhatsApp Business Manager

1. Go to [Meta Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Ensure you're in the correct Business Account

Show Image

---

### Step 2: Navigate to WhatsApp Accounts

1. On the left sidebar, scroll down to find **Accounts**
2. Click on **WhatsApp Accounts**
3. You'll see a list of your connected WhatsApp Business numbers

Show Image

---

### Step 3: Access Account Settings

1. Locate your WhatsApp Business Account
2. Click the **three dots (...)** next to your account name
3. From the dropdown menu, select **Manage Account Settings**

Show Image

---

### Step 4: Find Payment Settings

1. In the Account Settings page, scroll down
2. Look for the **Payment Settings** section
3. This section shows your current payment methods (if any)

Show Image

---

### Step 5: Add Payment Method

1. Click on **Add Payment Method** button
2. A payment method form will appear
3. Enter your card details:
   - **Card Number**
   - **Expiration Date** (MM/YY)
   - **CVV/CVC**
   - **Cardholder Name**
4. Fill in your billing address:
   - **Country/Region**
   - **Street Address**
   - **City**
   - **State/Province**
   - **Postal Code**

Show Image

---

### Step 6: Set as Default Payment Method

1. After adding your card, locate the payment method in the list
2. Click on the **three dots (...)** next to the card
3. Select **Set as Default Payment Method**
4. Confirm the action

Alternatively, when adding the card, you may see a checkbox:

- **Set as default payment method** - Check this box before saving
