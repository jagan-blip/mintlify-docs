## Filter Contacts

You can filter contacts in Turbodev to quickly find specific users based on defined criteria.

To apply filters:

- Click on the **Add Filter** option in the Contacts section
- Select the required filter condition
- Enter the relevant details and click **Apply**

  ![Contact Filter](</images/contact/contact filter.png>)

### Filter by Name

Useful when multiple contacts share similar names, helping you narrow down the exact contact.

### Filter by Marketing Opt-in

Allows you to separate contacts who have opted in or out of marketing communications.

### Filter by Tags

Filter contacts based on tags such as _New Enquiry_, _Purchased_, _Follow-up Required_, etc.

<Tip>
  You can apply **multiple filters simultaneously** to refine your search results. There is no limit to the number of filters you can use.
</Tip>

### Column Preferences

Turbodev allows you to customize how contact information is displayed.

![Contact Select Coloum](</images/contact/contact select coloum.png>)

Using **Column Preferences**, you can:

- Select which contact fields you want to view
- Rearrange columns based on your workflow
- Drag and drop fields to create a personalized contact view

  ![Contact Coloum](</images/contact/contact coloum.png>)

This helps Turbodev function as a **light CRM**, enabling teams to track customer details and personalize WhatsApp communication effectively.

<Note>
  Column preferences are applied **per user account** and do not affect other team members.
</Note>

### Search Contacts

You can quickly search contacts using the search bar in the Contacts section. By Contact name and Phone number (partial or full)
