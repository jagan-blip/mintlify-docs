### **Export Contacts from Turbodev**

You can export all contact records available in your Turbodev account with just a few clicks.

**To export contacts:**

- Go to the **Contacts** section in your Turbodev dashboard
- Click on the **Export** option
- Select **Export Contacts** from the dropdown
- Confirm the action to initiate the export

  ![Export](/images/contact/export.png)

To view your contact list open the downloaded file in your local device.
