### Overview

One of the powerful features offered by TurboDev is its **Broadcast functionality**.

If you’re looking to send announcements, offers, or important updates to a large group of contacts, TurboDev makes the process seamless. Creating and sending broadcast messages is intuitive, helping you engage your audience quickly and at scale.

### Broadcast Dashboard

The **Broadcast Dashboard** in TurboDev acts as a centralized space to create, manage, and track all your broadcast campaigns.

![Broadcast Dashboard](</images/broadcast/broadcast dashboard.png>)

From here, you can:

- Switch between available WhatsApp channels
- View your daily broadcast sending limits
- Monitor the quality rating of your WhatsApp number

### Create Your First Broadcast

To create your first broadcast, click on the **Create Broadcast** button located at the top-right corner of the screen and follow the guided steps to send your message.

![Create Broadcast](</images/broadcast/create broadcast.png>)
