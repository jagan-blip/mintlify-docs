Integrating Shopify with TurboDev enables you to automate customer communication across your entire e-commerce journey. From order confirmations and shipping updates to abandoned cart reminders and follow-ups, TurboDev ensures your customers receive timely, personalized WhatsApp messages automatically.

With this integration, you can reduce manual work, improve response time, and create a seamless post-purchase experience for your customers.

## Why Connect Shopify with TurboDev?

Connecting your Shopify store with TurboDev unlocks powerful automation capabilities designed for modern e-commerce businesses.

Once integrated, you can:

- Send automated WhatsApp order confirmations
- Notify customers about shipping and delivery updates
- Automate abandoned cart recovery messages
- Trigger follow-ups and customer engagement workflows
- Manage customer conversations from one unified dashboard

This helps improve customer experience, increase conversions, and save hours of manual effort.

## How Shopify Integration Works in TurboDev

The integration connects your Shopify store directly with TurboDev’s automation engine. When specific events occur in Shopify such as a new order, order update, or abandoned cart—TurboDev automatically triggers predefined workflows and sends WhatsApp messages to customers.

No coding is required. Everything works through TurboDev’s no-code automation builder.

## Step-by-Step Guide: Connect Shopify with TurboDev

Follow these simple steps to integrate your Shopify store.

### Step 1: Open Integrations in TurboDev

1. Log in to your **TurboDev dashboard**
2. Click on **Integrations** from the left navigation panel

   <Frame>
     ![Shopify](/images/Integration/shopify/shopify.png)
   </Frame>
3. Locate **Shopify** under Available Integrations
4. Click on **Shopify**

A connection pop-up will appear.

### Step 2: Connect Your Shopify Store

In the pop-up window, you will see:

**Connect Shopify Store**

Enter your Shopify store URL to connect your store and start sending order notifications.

<Frame>
  ![Shopify Connect](/images/Integration/shopify/shopify_connect.png)
</Frame>

Example:

```
your-store.myshopify.com
```

Now click:

**Connect Store**

### Step 3: Install TurboDev in Shopify

After clicking **Connect Store**, you will be redirected to your Shopify admin panel.

Here:

1. Review the TurboDev application permissions
2. Click **Install App**
3. Approve the installation

Once installed, your Shopify store will be securely connected to TurboDev.

You will automatically be redirected back to TurboDev.

### Step 4: Complete TurboDev Configuration

After installation, the **Configuration panel** will open in TurboDev.

You need to enter the following details:

**Required Fields:**

<Frame>
  ![Shopify Config](/images/Integration/shopify/shopify_config.png)
</Frame>

- Brand Name
- Public Shop URL (customer-facing store URL)
- Shop URL (your Shopify store URL)

Make sure all information is correct.

Now click:

**Update Configuration**

### Step 5: Access and Activate Shopify Workflows

Once configuration is completed successfully:

- Shopify integration will be activated
- Automation workflows will be unlocked
- You can start creating automated WhatsApp workflows

Your Shopify store is now fully connected with TurboDev.
